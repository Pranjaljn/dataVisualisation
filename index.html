REQUIRED VS IN POSITION

RATIO OF PHARMACISTS IN POSITION TO NEEDED

  • Most of the states have less number of pharmacists in position than needed.
  • Maharashtra and Odhisa seem to have good deal of governance, as despite being big states and high number of pharmacists needed, they have even more number of pharmacists in position.
  • Although Arunachal Pradesh needs large number of pharmacists relative to its population, there are not much pharmacists in position in comparison to its requirement.
  • Eastern states like Meghalaya, Tripura, Assam, Manipur are good in field of health awareness as they are above the unity bar in above representation.
  • 3 out of 6 union territories do not require any pharmacist, neither do they have any in position, and rest of them have sufficiently greater number of pharmacists in position than needed, which is also clear from above representations as all of them are above the unity bar
  • West Bengal and Rajasthan are again performing poor in medical field as they have extremely less number of pharmacists in position than needed.
